Koramangala Integrated Waste Management Center


Residents of koramangala have been segregating their waste since many years . However there was no processing facility in koramangala and the waste used to go miles away.
It was at that juncture that the RWAs came together and invited Carbon Masters to set up a Biomethanisation plant that could process all waste within the ward itself .

The biogas produced would then be piped to Truffles restaurant. Carbon Masters plant has helped in creating a 5 tpd decentralised processing in ward 151 and a circular economy.
This collaboration between BBMP , RWA and Carbon Masters has won an International award at Germany for the impact on waste at ward level. The plant plans to add another 5tpd capacity very soon.



Our Objective


Cleaner and greener environment through segregation of waste at source.

Reduced landfill space.

Creating sustainable livelihood among
waste collectors / waste handlers / waste pickers.
